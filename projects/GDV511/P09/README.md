Course: GDV 511 - Level Design, Spaces, and Encounter Craft

## Generic Description

Design objectives that can be completed in multiple orders; manage dependency and player planning; support meaningful route choice.

## Specific Execution

Build a mission with at least three objectives available in nonlinear order. At least one objective must change the state or difficulty of another.

## Grading Rubric

30 pts nonlinear structure; 20 pts dependency design; 20 pts route choice; 15 pts state communication; 15 pts documentation.
