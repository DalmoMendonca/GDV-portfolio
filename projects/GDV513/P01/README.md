## Generic Description

Transform source material into rules; distinguish representation from interaction; cite sources or influences responsibly.

## Specific Execution

Storytelling and Digital Humanities students adapt an archive item, historical episode, or literary fragment. Metamodern Interactive Art students create a ritualized interaction from a personal or cultural gesture. Gaming for the AI Age students use a small dataset or generated corpus as design material. The result must be playable without reading the source first.
