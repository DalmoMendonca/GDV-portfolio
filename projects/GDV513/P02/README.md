## Generic Description

Design point of view mechanically; make perspective affect available knowledge or actions; create empathy without forcing a conclusion.

## Specific Execution

Build a game where changing perspective changes rules, goals, language, sensory information, or agency. Include at least two perspectives and a moment where neither is complete alone.

## Grading Rubric

30 pts mechanical perspective design; 20 pts track-specific interpretation; 20 pts clarity of state changes; 15 pts ethical framing; 15 pts playtest notes.
