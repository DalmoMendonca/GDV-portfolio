## Generic Description

Design sources, sinks, scarcity, pricing, soft currency, hard currency, or non-monetary equivalents; prevent degenerate loops; communicate value without manipulative pressure.

## Specific Execution

Build a game with a closed or semi-closed economy involving at least three resources and two sinks. It may simulate premium currency, crafting value, faction influence, energy, or reputation, but no real purchases are allowed. Include a balance sheet showing expected flow per minute or turn.

## Grading Rubric

30 pts economy model; 20 pts balance; 20 pts player decision quality; 15 pts value communication; 15 pts balance and ethics documentation.
