Course: GDV 623 - Track Studio II: Advanced Authorship

Public repository target: GDV623-P01-argument-game

## Generic Description

Make a claim through mechanics; support interpretation with evidence; allow players to test the claim.

## Specific Execution

Storytelling and Digital Humanities students build a playable humanities argument. Metamodern Interactive Art students build a game that oscillates between sincerity and critique. Gaming for the AI Age students build a game that argues about AI capability, labor, risk, or creativity. Include an author's statement.

## Grading Rubric

30 pts strength of playable argument; 20 pts track sophistication; 20 pts player agency; 15 pts supporting statement; 15 pts playtest response.
