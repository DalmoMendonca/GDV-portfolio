## Generic Description

Adapt a non-game form into play; decide what to preserve, cut, or transform; justify adaptation choices.

## Specific Execution

Adapt a poem, essay, performance, dataset, model behavior, painting, oral history, myth, or public document into a playable game. Include an adaptation note identifying three major transformations.

## Grading Rubric

30 pts adaptation quality; 20 pts transformation rationale; 20 pts playability; 15 pts respect for source context; 15 pts notes.
