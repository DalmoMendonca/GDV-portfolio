## Generic Description

Gather and triage community feedback; distinguish taste from usability; make a revision plan.

## Specific Execution

Publish or privately distribute a game to a small community. Collect at least 10 feedback items, categorize them, implement at least three changes, and justify three rejected changes.

## Grading Rubric

30 pts feedback process; 20 pts revision quality; 20 pts triage reasoning; 15 pts community communication; 15 pts reflection.
