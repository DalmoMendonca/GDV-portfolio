## Generic Description

Show how a design changes under two interpretations; compare mechanics; make contrast playable.

## Specific Execution

Build one game with two modes, two versions, or two playable stances. The difference must represent a meaningful theoretical, aesthetic, cultural, or AI-system comparison.

## Grading Rubric

30 pts comparative design; 20 pts clarity of contrast; 20 pts playability of both versions; 15 pts analysis; 15 pts polish.
